About SISILA OPTICALS

Who We Are

Serving Akuressa
with Pride & Precision

SISILA OPTICALS has been the cornerstone of eye health in Akuressa since our founding. Nestled on Kamburupitiya Road, we started as a small family-run shop and have grown into a full-service optical center trusted by thousands across the Matara district.

We believe that clear vision is not a luxury — it is a right. Our mission has always been to deliver professional optical care at accessible prices, ensuring that every person, from schoolchildren to seniors, can see the world at its best.

"Clear vision is the first step to a brighter future."

Our team of certified optometrists and trained optical technicians work with cutting-edge diagnostic tools to provide accurate prescriptions and personalized eyewear solutions. Whether you're a first-time glasses wearer or looking to upgrade your lenses, we're here every step of the way.

What We Do

Our Services

01
🔬
Eye Examinations

Comprehensive Vision Testing

Our eye examinations go far beyond simply reading a chart. Using advanced autorefractors, slit-lamp biomicroscopy, and digital fundus imaging, our certified optometrists assess your full ocular health — detecting conditions like myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, glaucoma, and more at their earliest stages.

Each examination includes a detailed consultation where your optometrist explains the findings clearly and recommends the best path forward — whether that's spectacles, contact lenses, or referral to a specialist.

Visual Acuity Test Refraction Assessment Intraocular Pressure Retinal Examination Colour Vision Test
02
👓
Prescription Glasses

Custom Lenses, Perfect Fit

Every pair of prescription glasses we make is crafted to your exact measurements. We offer single-vision, bifocal, progressive, and occupational lenses in a wide range of materials including polycarbonate, high-index, and photochromic options.

Our in-store technicians use precision edging equipment to cut and fit lenses perfectly into your chosen frame, with most pairs ready within 24–48 hours.

Single Vision Bifocal Lenses Progressive Lenses Anti-Reflective Coating Blue Light Filter Photochromic
03
🕶️
Sunglasses

Style Meets Sun Protection

Sri Lanka's tropical sun demands serious UV protection. Our sunglasses collection combines 100% UV400 protection with contemporary designs — from polarized lenses that cut glare to gradient tints for everyday style.

We stock a carefully curated range of sunwear for men, women, and children, with options for prescription sun lenses as well.

UV400 Protection Polarized Lenses Prescription Sunglasses Sports Sunwear
04
🟤
Contact Lenses

Freedom Without Frames

Contact lenses offer a glasses-free lifestyle without compromising on clarity. Our contact lens fitting service is thorough and patient — we take time to ensure your lenses fit comfortably and your vision is perfectly corrected.

We carry daily disposables, monthly lenses, toric lenses for astigmatism, and multifocal contacts from leading brands.

Daily Disposables Monthly Lenses Toric (Astigmatism) Multifocal Contacts Colour Contacts
05
🔧
Frame Repairs

Back to Perfect in No Time

Accidents happen. Our technicians can repair most frame damage quickly and affordably — from replacing a missing screw to repairing broken bridges and adjusting bent arms. We handle both plastic and metal frames with equal expertise.

Screw Replacement Frame Straightening Nose Pad Replacement Hinge Repair
06
👶
Kids Eyewear

Gentle Care for Little Eyes

Children's vision needs are unique. Our pediatric eye examinations are designed to be gentle, engaging, and effective — detecting issues like amblyopia (lazy eye) and strabismus early, when treatment is most effective.

We stock a fun collection of durable, flexible frames in bright colors and character designs that children love to wear.

Pediatric Eye Tests Flexible Frames Impact-Resistant Lenses Ages 3 and Above
